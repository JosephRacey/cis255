Useful HTML tags

Introduction The purpose of this assignment is to talk about my three favorite HTML tags. I have prepared this HTML documant to demonstrate my understanding of making a basic HTML web page and discuss the three tags that I currently favor.

Bullet Lists <ul> I think that a good bullet list can be useful on any page to convey a few short bits of information in a more concise and organized form. A few examples would be,

  • This list of examples.
  • Goals of a company on their web page
  • Products or services that a company provides
  • Locations that the buisiness operates at
  • A tech savvy dad's shopping list
Eveyone has seen bullet lists before and it is undeniable that they indeed grab the attention of the reader.

Horizontal Rule <hr> The horizontal rule tag has quickly become one of my favorite tags, I haven't done much HTML but I like a very structured and organized page. I agree with Dr. Corser that it is a great tool for organizing web pages. I just really like the way that it seprates my thoughts.

Image <img> Everyone like a page full of flashy images right? What good is any website if there isn't anything interesting to look at other than text? For this reason the IMG tag is one of my favorites.

Conclusion In conclusion, even though I don't have much experience with HTML, I have chosen my favorite HTML tags so far. They may not be the same as everybodys, but I think that everybody would agree that they are useful.
